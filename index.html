About Saveetha Engineering College

Saveetha Engineering College (SEC), a constituent of Saveetha Institute of Medical and Technical Sciences (SIMATS), is an autonomous institution founded in 2001. It is approved by AICTE and affiliated with Anna University. SEC is dedicated to providing quality education in various engineering disciplines, fostering innovation, and promoting research.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a vibrant campus life, SEC aims to nurture young talents into competent and responsible engineers ready to meet global challenges.

Our Departments

Computer Science & Engineering

Focuses on algorithms, data structures, software development, and AI.

Electronics & Communication Engineering

Deals with electronics, telecommunications, signal processing, and embedded systems.

Mechanical Engineering

Covers design, analysis, manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems.

Civil Engineering

Involves planning, design, construction, and maintenance of physical and naturally built environment.

Information Technology

Emphasizes on computer software, hardware, and networking for business and other uses.

Electrical & Electronics Engineering

Studies and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism.
